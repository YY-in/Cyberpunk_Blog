## Overview

Explored the practical value of MR technology in daily life scenarios. Built a Mixed Reality cooking instruction prototype using Unity and Meta Quest 3, aiming to solve the pain points of traditional cooking education — unintuitive steps and interrupted operations — through a blended virtual-real interaction approach.

## Responsibilities

- Completed MR scene construction, spatially overlaying virtual chef imagery with real kitchen environments and performing positioning calibration
- Participated in qualitative user research, collecting core pain points in users' cooking learning experience through interviews and observation

## Technical Specs

| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| Engine | Unity |

## Key Features

- Virtual chef character overlaid in real kitchen space with spatial calibration
- Step-by-step cooking guidance without interrupting physical workflow
- User research driven design iterations based on qualitative findings
